Frequently Asked Questions

Feel free to ask us whatever question(s) you may have.

  • What does a pharmacist do?

    A pharmacist is a healthcare professional who specializes in the use and safety of medications. They dispense prescription medications to patients and offer advice on their safe use.

  • Can I take these two medications together?

    Pharmacists are experts in drug interactions and can provide advice on whether certain medications can be safely taken together.

  • What are the potential side effects of this medication?

    Pharmacists can provide information on the potential side effects of a medication and offer advice on what to do if these side effects occur.

  • How should I take this medication?

    Pharmacists can provide instructions on the best way to take a medication to ensure its effectiveness.

  • Can I drink alcohol while taking this medication?

    Pharmacists can advise on whether it's safe to consume alcohol while taking a specific medication.
